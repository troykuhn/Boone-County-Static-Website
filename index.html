May 21, 2020 - Donald Lawon, President of the Boone County Board of Commissioners, is authorizing voters to enter through the east side entrance (Meridian Street) of the courthouse for voting purposes only beginning on Tuesday, May 26th through Saturday, May 30th. Voters will not be required to submit to health screening when utilizing this entrance but will be restricted from going into other parts of the courthouse
